Nupoor Gandhi

I am a first-year student at Heinz College, Carnegie Mellon University, where I am pursuing a joint PhD in Machine Learning and Public Policy. Currently, I am working with Yulia Tsvetkov and Alex Choulechova on building robust NLU, specifically in child welfare and medical domains. Previously, I received my bachelors in Computer Science with a minor in Math at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. At Illinois I worked on modeling public health outcomes using social media data as a part of the Text Information Management and Analysis Group and the Health and Social Media Group under the supervision of ChengXiang Zhai and Dolores Albarracin. I was also a Data Science for Social Good Fellow at Imperial College London, where I worked on a QA system for nonprofit Barefoot Law to accelerate access to legal advice.
